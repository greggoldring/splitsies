Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Splitsies: Track Cycling Splits (“the App”) is published by Gregg Goldring. This policy describes how the App handles your information.

Data collection

We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data or information about you. The App does not use analytics, advertising, or any third-party services that collect user data.

Data stored on your device

Stopwatch times and race history are stored only on your device using the App’s local storage. This data is never sent to our servers or any third party because the App has no servers and does not transmit data off your device.

Children’s privacy

The App does not collect any information from anyone, including children. No personal information is requested or stored.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or the App, you may contact the developer at the support contact provided in the App Store listing for Splitsies: Track Cycling Splits.
